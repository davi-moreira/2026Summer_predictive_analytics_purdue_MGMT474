4. Peer Review Rubric

Why Peer Review?

‚ÄúYou learn as much from reviewing others‚Äô work as from your own.‚Äù
Good feedback requires critical thinking and makes you a better analyst.

4.1 Review Principles

Be useful, not nice: - Focus on specific, actionable feedback - Point out both strengths and weaknesses - Suggest concrete improvements

Be professional: - Critique the work, not the person - Use ‚ÄúI notice‚Äù instead of ‚ÄúYou did wrong‚Äù - Balance critical feedback with positive observations

Be thorough: - Check technical correctness - Evaluate communication clarity - Consider business relevance

4.2 Peer Review Rubric
